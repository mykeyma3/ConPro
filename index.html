How to Tag Your Items

In PlanSwift, append each item's name with one of the following tags:

  • Door Units Call – UDU
  • Unit Hardware Call – UDH
  • Bath Hardware – UBH
  • Shower Door – TSD
  • Mirrors – TUM
  • Millwork – TUT
  • Shelving – TUS
  • Window Blinds – UWB

Example: R-01 4A - UDU
